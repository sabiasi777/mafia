Game Rules

Mafia is a game of deception and deduction. Each player is assigned a role...

Roles

  • Mafia: Eliminate other players without being discovered.
  • Detective: Investigate players to find out if they are mafia.
  • Doctor: Protect one player per night from being killed.
  • Villager: Try to identify the mafia and vote them out.
